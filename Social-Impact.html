A Story

From training floor to country lead.

When we onboarded the local team for a manufacturing client in Bekasi, one of the first hires was a 26-year-old production supervisor with no cross-border experience.

Three years later, after the capability transfer that's built into every engagement, she runs the entire Indonesian operation and reports directly to the parent company's board. That's the whole idea: the foreign company gets a business that works, and Indonesia gets a leader who will build the next ten.

The metric we care about most isn't revenue. It's how many Indonesians are running things we helped start.
