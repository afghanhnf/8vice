Why We Exist

Born from a problem we kept watching repeat.

Over a decade in Jakarta, our founders watched the same story play out again and again. A capable Chinese company - strong product, real capital, genuine ambition - would land in Indonesia, sign a glossy market-entry report, and then stall. The report was never wrong. It was just never built by anyone who had to live with the consequences.

Licensing took twice as long as promised. The distribution partner was the wrong one. The local team was hired for résumés, not for the actual market. By the time the headquarters in Shanghai or Shenzhen understood why, a year and a budget were gone.

8vice was built to be the firm we wished those companies had called first: senior operators who give honest reads, stay through execution, and treat your capital as if it were ours.

"We only take work we'd stake our own name on."

When I started advising Chinese companies in Indonesia, I made a rule that still defines us: if I would not put my own money behind the move, I tell the client - even if it costs us the engagement. That honesty is the whole product.

Indonesia rewards patience and punishes shortcuts. Our job is to compress your learning curve without pretending the shortcuts exist.

Our Story

How we got here.

From a two-person advisory in a Pancoran co-working space to a network of senior operators across sixteen industries. A short version of a long road.

2018

The first call

Founded in Jakarta to help a single Chinese payments company untangle Indonesian licensing. The engagement worked - and the phone kept ringing.

2020

From advisor to network

Formalised our model: a curated bench of senior operators, matched to each client's industry instead of a fixed hierarchy.

2022

Training & Insight launch

Added capability-building and a free Insight practice - podcasts and field reports - to lift the whole corridor, not only paying clients.

2024

Sixteen industries deep

Coverage expanded across consumer, fintech, manufacturing, energy and beyond as Indonesia became China's #1 ASEAN trade partner.

2026

The corridor standard

120+ engagements delivered and a subscription training product in market. The bridge is now a body of work.
