# Product Requirements Document (PRD)

## Project Overview
* **Project Name:** B2B Strategic Partner Corporate Website - Secondary Pages Expansion
* **Target Audience:** Enterprise Clients, C-Level Executives, International Cross-Border Stakeholders (specifically Indonesia-China trade/investment corridor).
* **Core Principle:** Anti-Gravity Approach (Straightforward, zero fluff, maximum clarity, highly structured, and data-grounded).

---

## 1. Global Visual & Technical Framework
To ensure absolute consistency with the existing landing page, all secondary pages must strictly adhere to the following UI/UX guardrails:

### Color Palette
* **Primary Background:** Deep Dark Mode (`#0A0A0A` to `#121212`).
* **Section Breakers:** Clean White (`#FFFFFF`) blocks used sparingly for high-contrast informational shifts to prevent dark-mode fatigue.
* **Accent Color:** High-visibility Red (`#E60000` or `#FF0000`) restricted to Call-to-Actions (CTA), critical metrics, active navigation states, and crucial headers.
* **Typography Colors:** Primary Headings (`#FFFFFF`), Body Copy / Subtext (`#CCCCCC` or `#A3A3A3` for optimal contrast and readability).

### Typography & Structure
* **Font Styling:** Clean, professional sans-serif. Utilize inline typographic contrast by pairing Regular/Light text with **Bold/Heavy** text within the same header phrase (e.g., "What We Do **Passionately**?").
* **Layout Design:** 1200px - 1440px max-width container. Embody a geometric, analitis feel using subtle background grid lines and thin structural dividers (`border: 1px solid #222222`).
* **Component Styling:** Smooth interactive transitions. Interactive cards and main buttons must feature subtle rounded corners (`border-radius: 6px` to `8px`).

---

## 2. Page-by-Page Technical & Content Specifications

### A. Our Services (Detail Pages)
*Applies individually to: Market Intelligence, Consultancy, Training*

* **Objective:** Unpack the deep vertical expertise of each service line and drive qualified lead generation.
* **Section & Layout Architecture:**
  1. **Hero Section (50/50 Horizontal Split):**
     * **Left Column:** Explicit service title with contrasting bold styling and a 2-line strategic overview.
     * **Right Column:** High-end static abstract 3D asset or dark-themed data visual component (e.g., glowing node webs for Consultancy; micro-charts for Market Intelligence).
  2. **Core Capabilities Grid (3-Column Layout):**
     * A minimalist grid showcasing sub-capabilities.
     * **Card Component:** Transparent background with a thin boundary line. Features a custom red line-art icon at the top, a bold header, and a highly concise bulleted summary.
  3. **Methodology Workflow (Linear Process):**
     * A horizontal or vertical stepped guide (Step 01, Step 02, Step 03) connected by a faint red dotted path. Emphasizes structured delivery over abstract promises.
  4. **Section CTA (Centered Banner):**
     * High-impact text block: "Ready to accelerate your growth? Let’s talk strategy."
     * **Action Element:** Solid red button text [Consult with Our Strategists].

### B. Clients Page
* **Objective:** Showcase global industry enterprise relationships and solidify corporate trust.
* **Section & Layout Architecture:**
  1. **Introductory Header:** Centered minimalist text: "Trusted by **Industry Leaders** Across Regions."
  2. **Interactive Brand Grid (4 or 5-Column Matrix):**
     * **Visual Styling:** All client logos must be displayed in a uniform monochrome light gray format to preserve dark-mode visual unity.
     * **Hover Effect:** Smooth transition revealing the original corporate brand color on desktop cursor hover.
     * **Functional Filter:** Tabbed categorization system above the grid allowing users to filter by industry vertical (e.g., *All / Logistics / Energy & Tech / Cross-Border / Retail*).
  3. **Executive Testimonial Carousel (Full-Width Slider):**
     * Dark tinted container showing a high-level quote text in large italics.
     * Includes client photo/avatar, C-suite title, and an embedded corporate logo. Background utilizes a faint, oversized double-quotation mark watermark.

### C. Case Study Directory & Details
* **Objective:** Prove systemic business impact using undeniable, data-driven outcomes.
* **Section & Layout Architecture:**
  1. **Hero Section:** Left-aligned bold heading: "Proven Outcomes. **Real Impact.**"
  2. **Featured Case Study (Full-Width Highlight Card):**
     * **Left Split:** Client name, macro-metric indicator in massive bold red typography (e.g., **+$130M Market Cap Expansion**), and problem/solution brief.
     * **Right Split:** Contextual industrial/corporate documentary photograph.
     * **Action Element:** Text button with arrow indicator `[Read Full Analysis ->]`.
  3. **Historical Case Matrix (2-Column Grid):**
     * Standard cards sorting older projects.
     * **Card Meta Data:** Red category tag, project name, 3 hard-metric bulleted results, and a flat border button.

### D. Article (Insights & Business Intelligence)
* **Objective:** Position the enterprise as the premier thought leader on macroeconomic trends and regional trade shifts.
* **Section & Layout Architecture:**
  1. **Featured Editorial Hero:** Full-width container showing the latest critical whitepaper or article. Uses a dark gradient overlay extending from the bottom up to ensure title readability against the background imagery.
  2. **Two-Column Knowledge Stream (60/40 Split):**
     * **Left Stream (60% Width):** Infinite scroll or cleanly paginated vertical stack of articles. Each row contains a thumbnail, date metadata, calculated reading time, title, and excerpt.
     * **Right Sidebar (40% Width):** Sticky column containing a keyword search bar, explicit category filters (e.g., Macro Economy, Trade Regulations, Supply Chain Optimization), and a quick email newsletter signup box labeled "Field Notes from the Frontier".

### E. Career Page
* **Objective:** Source top-tier advisory talent capable of operating in highly complex cross-border environments.
* **Section & Layout Architecture:**
  1. **Hero Introduction:** Centered bold text: "Shape the Future of **Global Enterprise**." Subtle video loop of a sleek, dark corporate environment playing in the background.
  2. **Core Values Block (3-Column Layout):**
     * Icons highlighting structural principles: *Global Mindset, Absolute Integrity, Data Over Intuition*.
  3. **Open Vacancies Interface (Structured Data Table):**
     * Clean grid matrix displaying clear row fields: *Role Title | Department | Geographic Location (e.g., Jakarta, Beijing, Remote)*.
     * **Interaction:** Clicking any row smoothly accordions downwards to reveal requirements and an immediate inline application form hook.

### F. Contact Page (Conversion Engine)
* **Objective:** Maximize high-intent corporate inquiries while entirely minimizing friction.
* **Section & Layout Architecture:**
  1. **Split-Screen Console Layout (Above-the-Fold - Zero Scrolling Required):**
     * **Left Panel (Global Presence Data):** Title reads "Let’s Start a Strategic Conversation." Lists physical headquarters details (Jakarta HQ, China Regional Office) complete with encrypted telephone channels, direct email endpoints, and a stylized monochrome vectorized regional map component.
     * **Right Panel (Minimalist Inbound Form):**
       * Clean field inputs featuring bottom-borders only (`border-bottom: 2px solid #333333; border-top:0; border-left:0; border-right:0`). Focus states shift the border line to bright red.
       * **Required Fields:** Full Name, Corporate Email Endpoint, Company Name, Scope of Interest (Dropdown select matching sub-services), and Message Brief.
       * **Action Button:** Full-width solid red submit element `[Initiate Corporate Briefing]`.
